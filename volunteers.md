## What Can I Expect?

### Who Can Join?

Volunteers of all experience are welcome, whether you're a seasoned jazz soloist
or a beginner. Because our repertoire currently focuses on jazz and traditional
pop standards, volunteers should be comfortable following sheet music and/or
chord sheets, depending on the song. However, this is the only real requirement.

These performances are a very casual, low-stakes environment. If you are looking
to gain experience playing in a group, have fun playing some music, or to just
socialize, you are welcome to join!

### Scheduling

As a group, we typically schedule performances with care facilities on a
**monthly** basis.

Once we get confirmation from each of the facilities we plan on performing at,
we will reach out to each volunteer with a setlist (typically including lead
sheets or chord sheets) and organize an **optional rehearsal**.

### Rehearsals

We typically run a short rehearsal a few days in advance of each performance.
These rehearsals are optional, but they are a good opportunity to practice as a
group and ask questions.

**Don't worry about learning every song on the list, especially on such short
notice. Just learn the songs that interest you!**

### Performances

Our performances are typically 35-50 minutes long (roughly 6-10 songs.) These
performances are a casual, relaxed environment, and as stated previously, we do
not expect you to play every single song in the set list.

On performance days, volunteers typically arrive a little early (10-15 minutes)
to set up and run through any details before we begin.

### How Often Do I Have to Perform?

There is no minimum number of performances that volunteers must attend. Whether
you join us regularly or just for a single performance, your contribution will
be greatly appreciated!
