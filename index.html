Volunteer Performers'
Collective

Started June 2025 by two friends with a passion for music, the Volunteer Performers Collective was created with the hopes of bringing others with the same passion for music to places in need of entertainment! We provide youth with opportunities to deepen their interest in music in a supportive casual environment while doing something for their community on the side.
